07

UX Principles

Core design philosophy guiding every interaction

‚ö°

Instant Feedback

Every action gets immediate visual and haptic response. Check-ins animate instantly with optimistic UI. Users never wait or wonder if their action registered. Animations complete in under 300ms to feel snappy.

üéØ

3-Tap Rule

Any core action is achievable within 3 taps. Opening the app ‚Üí checking in a habit is 1 tap. Adding a new habit is 3 taps. No buried menus or complex navigation. Direct access to what matters.

üéâ

Celebration-Driven

Progress is rewarded with delight. Streaks get fire emojis. Milestones trigger confetti. Completions animate with satisfaction. The app celebrates users' wins, big and small, creating positive reinforcement loops.

üå±

Progressive Disclosure

Advanced features reveal themselves when needed. New users see simple interfaces. Power users discover analytics, AI insights, and customization gradually. Complexity scales with user expertise.

üé®

Joyful Minimalism

Clean layouts with personality. Generous white space lets content breathe. Color is purposeful, not decorative. Every element earns its place. The design is minimal but never boring or sterile.

‚ôø

Accessible by Default

Large touch targets (44√ó44pt minimum). High contrast ratios (4.5:1+). Dynamic type support. Screen reader labels. Color isn't the only indicator - we use shapes, icons, and text too. Everyone can build better habits.
